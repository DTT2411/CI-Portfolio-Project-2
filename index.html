Rock Paper Scissors Spock Lizard

Description

"Rock Paper Scissors Spock Lizard" is an extension of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game. There are two additional gestures - Spock and Lizard - with their own interactions with the original three gestures. Read the rules below to find out the weaknesses and strengths of each gesture, then click one of the buttons below to start a match against the computer.

Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors and Lizard
  • Scissors beats Paper and Lizard
  • Paper beats Rock and Spock
  • Spock beats Scissors and Rock
  • Lizard beats Spock and Paper
